MOXI CORP WEBSITE UPLOAD INSTRUCTIONS

Rename and upload these files to the root of your website:
- landing(1).html -> index.html
- styles(25).css -> styles.css
- script(17).js -> script.js
- animations(3).js -> animations.js (optional)
- thanks.html
- thanks.css
- thanks.js

Also upload your existing images folder. The landing page expects:
- images/program-hero.jpg
- images/vcard-qr.png
- images/newlogo2.svg
- images/ashar-music.png
- images/Web1.png
- images/web5.png
- images/web7.png

The only clickable link on the page is:
- Back to Moxi Corp -> index.html

To redirect Formspree submissions to this page:
1. Publish thanks.html first.
2. Open your Formspree form.
3. Open the form Settings.
4. Set the custom Thank You redirect to the full public URL, for example:
   https://moxicorpstudio.com.au/thanks.html

The custom redirect option depends on the Formspree plan/settings available on your account.

After upload, check the home page on a phone and desktop, test every portfolio
link, scan the QR code, and send one test Formspree enquiry.
