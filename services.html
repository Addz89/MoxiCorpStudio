Mobile Optimisation

Most people will judge your business from their phone first. If your mobile layout is weak, your business looks weak. Harsh, but true.

  • Responsive layouts for phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops.
  • Clear mobile navigation, readable sections, and tap-friendly buttons.
  • Mobile-first service sections that get to the point quickly.

SEO Foundations

A website needs structure that Google can understand. SEO is not magic; it starts with clean pages, headings, content, speed, and metadata.

  • Page titles, descriptions, headings, and clear content hierarchy.
  • Service-focused sections written so visitors and search engines understand the offer.
  • Google Search Console and Analytics guidance where needed.

  • Content that helps visitors understand what to do next.
