01 / Our approach

Design starts with the customer journey.

Many small-business websites are treated like online brochures: a logo, a few photos, a short paragraph and a contact form. That puts a business online, but it does not automatically explain why someone should choose it.

Moxi Corp takes a more structured approach. Each page is planned around what the customer needs to understand, what proof they need to see and what action they should take next.
