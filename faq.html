Project basics

Payments and Stripe

Yes. Secure Stripe checkout can be connected to package buttons or payment links so clients can pay online without complicated back-and-forth arrangements.

Payment terms depend on the project. Smaller fixed-price packages may be paid upfront, while larger custom projects may use a deposit and final-payment arrangement. The agreed terms are confirmed before work begins.

The package covers the agreed website build. Extra pages, additional design work, logo creation, maintenance, paid services, domains and hosting are quoted separately when required.
