The build process

Six focused stages. No unnecessary confusion.

Every stage has a clear purpose and outcome, so decisions are made before they become expensive changes.

01 / Discover

Understand the business

We discuss your services, customers, competitors, content, goals and the result the website needs to create.

02 / Scope

Confirm the project

The package, pages, features, responsibilities, price and delivery expectations are written down clearly.

03 / Plan

Map the customer journey

The page structure, content flow and calls to action are planned before visual design begins.

04 / Build

Design and develop

Your responsive website is created around the approved direction with practical feedback points.

05 / Test

Check the details

Layouts, navigation, links, forms, content and key screen sizes are checked before launch.

06 / Launch

Connect and go live

The website is connected to your domain, final checks are completed and support is provided for handover.

What we need from you

Good input creates a stronger website.

  • Logo, brand assets, photos and existing content
