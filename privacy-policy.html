Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Moxi Corp Studio ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, process, and safeguard your data when you visit our website (moxicorpstudio.com.au) or engage our web development and digital services. Our practices comply with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) as defined in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that is necessary to deliver our premium digital services. The types of data we may collect include:

  • Personal Identifiers: Your full name, company name, email address, and phone number when you fill out a contact form or initiate a project checkout.
  • Financial Information: Billing details and payment information. Note: All direct payment processing is handled securely via Stripe. We do not store or host your raw credit card data on our servers.
  • Technical & Usage Data: IP addresses, browser types, device information, and interaction metrics collected automatically via standard analytics tools to optimize website performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is strictly used to execute our services and improve our platform. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Communicate with you regarding project inquiries, quotes, and web development timelines.
  • Process financial transactions and deliver automated invoices securely via Stripe.
  • Provide ongoing maintenance, security updates, and retainer support for your digital assets.
  • Analyze website traffic to improve user experience and ensure optimal load times.

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement high-end security measures to protect your data. Your project submissions and contact information are transmitted over secure, encrypted connections (SSL/TLS). Our platform is hosted on modern infrastructure via Vercel, utilizing enterprise-grade serverless environments to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or data breaches.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

To provide our premium architecture, we partner with industry-leading third-party services. These partners only process data according to strict security standards:

  • Stripe: Utilized for secure payment processing and financial routing.
  • Formspree / Vercel Serverless: Utilized for securely routing contact and project checkout data.
  • Google Analytics: Utilized to track anonymous, aggregated performance metrics.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to outside marketing or advertising agencies.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website utilizes cookies—small text files stored on your device—to ensure site functionality and track user sessions securely. You have the ability to decline or manage cookie preferences through your web browser settings; however, doing so may limit your ability to use certain features of our platform.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act, you have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to ask for corrections if the data is inaccurate, out of date, or incomplete. If you wish to review, update, or request the deletion of your personal file, please contact us using the details below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy as our technology stack evolves or as required by Australian law. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
