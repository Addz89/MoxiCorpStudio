Privacy and information use

Privacy Policy.

This policy explains how Moxi Corp handles information submitted through this website.

  • Last updated 16 July 2026
  • Business Moxi Corp
  • Contact support@moxicorpstudio.com.au
Plain-language summary

Your information is used to respond to your enquiry.

Moxi Corp does not sell personal information. Details submitted through the website are used for project communication, quoting and related business administration.

01 / Information collected

What may be collected

When you submit an enquiry, Moxi Corp may collect your name, email address, phone number, business name, website address, project requirements, budget or package preference and any information included in your message.

02 / How it is used

Why the information is needed

Information is used to respond to enquiries, understand project requirements, prepare quotes, communicate about services, administer approved projects and meet reasonable business or legal obligations.

03 / Service providers

Forms and website tools

The enquiry form is processed using Formspree. Website hosting, analytics or email providers may process limited technical or contact information as part of delivering their services. Their own privacy terms apply to their platforms.

04 / Security and retention

Reasonable protection

Reasonable steps are taken to protect submitted information. No internet transmission is completely risk-free. Information is kept only for as long as reasonably required for enquiries, projects, records and legal obligations.

05 / Access and contact

Your questions

You may request access to, correction of or deletion of personal information held by Moxi Corp where applicable. Email support@moxicorpstudio.com.au with your request or privacy question.
