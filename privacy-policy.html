Information we may collect

When you submit a contact form, quote request or project enquiry, Moxi Corp may collect information you choose to provide.

  • Your name and email address
  • Your phone number, when supplied
  • Your business name and website details
  • Your selected package or service

How your information is used

Submitted information is used to respond to enquiries, prepare quotes, discuss website projects, provide requested services and communicate about your project.

Moxi Corp does not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third-party services

This website may use third-party services to process forms, payments, website hosting and analytics. These providers may process information according to their own privacy policies.

  • Formspree may process website form submissions
  • Stripe may process secure payments
  • GitHub Pages or another hosting provider may deliver website files

Data security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect information submitted through this website. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.
